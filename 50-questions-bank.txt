═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
50-QUESTION INTERVIEW BANK — SOUL CALIBRATION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Usage: React randomly selects 5. Each question maps to specific 
soul_profile fields. Categorized by target schema section.
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

GROUP 1: LEXICON & SYNTAX
Targets: greeting_taxonomy, sign_off_taxonomy, primary_idioms,
         profanity_index, emoji_to_text_ratio
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Q1  | When they pick up the phone or text you first thing in the 
    | morning, what are their exact words?
Q2  | How do they usually say goodbye or sign off at the end of 
    | a long conversation?
Q3  | What is a catchphrase, idiom, or weird slang word they use 
    | constantly?
Q4  | If they stub their toe or drop their phone, what exact curse 
    | words (or substitute words) fly out of their mouth?
Q5  | Do they use emojis, or are they strictly a "text-only, proper 
    | punctuation" kind of person? Which emojis do they abuse?
Q6  | When they are absolutely shocked or surprised by news, what 
    | is their go-to reaction phrase?
Q7  | How do they refer to you? Do they use your real name, a 
    | specific nickname, or an insult-as-a-term-of-endearment?
Q8  | If they are running 10 minutes late, what text do they send 
    | you?
Q9  | When they agree with you, do they say "Yes," "Absolutely," 
    | "For sure," or something else?
Q10 | What is a movie quote, TV line, or song lyric they quote way 
    | too often?

GROUP 2: HUMOR & JOY
Targets: humor_mechanisms, emotional_baseline_valence
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Q11 | When they make a joke, are they usually making fun of 
    | themselves, making fun of you, or pointing out something 
    | absurd in the room?
Q12 | What is the fastest way to make them laugh until they can't 
    | breathe?
Q13 | Describe their "Dad joke" or "Mom joke" style. Do they love 
    | terrible puns?
Q14 | If someone trips and falls (but isn't hurt), do they gasp and 
    | help, or laugh hysterically?
Q15 | How do they react to heavy sarcasm? Do they dish it back, or 
    | do they take it literally?
Q16 | If they try to pull a prank or be mischievous, what is their 
    | usual tactic?
Q17 | What is a topic they find inexplicably hilarious that no one 
    | else really gets?
Q18 | When they are in an incredibly good mood, how does their 
    | behavior change?
Q19 | Do they ever use self-deprecation to defuse a tense 
    | situation? Give an example.
Q20 | How do they tease the people they love the most?

GROUP 3: FRICTION & STRESS
Targets: conflict_resolution_style, passive_aggressive_markers,
         anxiety_markers, emotional_volatility_index,
         disagreement_framing
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Q21 | If you cancel plans on them at the last minute, exactly what 
    | text message do they send back?
Q22 | When they are secretly mad at you, how do you know? (What is 
    | their passive-aggressive tell?)
Q23 | In a heated argument, do they yell and explode, or go ice-
    | cold and silent?
Q24 | If they are stressed about money or work, what is the phrase 
    | they repeat to themselves (or you)?
Q25 | How do they tell you that you are doing something wrong or 
    | making a mistake?
Q26 | When they know they are wrong, how do they apologize? (Or do 
    | they just act like nothing happened?)
Q27 | What is a minor, petty inconvenience that completely ruins 
    | their day?
Q28 | If you give them a compliment they don't know how to handle, 
    | how do they deflect it?
Q29 | When they are worried about your safety or health, how does 
    | it manifest? (Nagging, hovering, silence?)
Q30 | How quickly do they forgive and forget after a fight?

GROUP 4: AFFECTION & EMPATHY
Targets: empathy_quotient, affection_delivery_mechanism,
         affirmation_delivery_style, advice_giving_tendency
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Q31 | When you are going through a terrible breakup or job loss, 
    | what is their exact method of comforting you?
Q32 | Do they say "I love you" out loud, or do they show it by 
    | fixing your car / cooking you food?
Q33 | When you tell them a crazy story about your day, how do they 
    | actively listen? (e.g., "No way!", "Uh huh", giving instant 
    | advice)
Q34 | If you have a problem, do they immediately try to solve it 
    | with advice, or just listen to you vent?
Q35 | How do they express pride in your accomplishments without 
    | sounding cheesy?
Q36 | What is the most thoughtful, specific gift they have ever 
    | given you or someone else?
Q37 | Do they randomly check in on you? What does that text 
    | usually look like?
Q38 | How do they act around strangers or service workers compared 
    | to how they act around you?
Q39 | If someone else is crying in the room, what is their 
    | immediate physical reaction?
Q40 | What is a piece of life advice they have given you more than 
    | three times?

GROUP 5: QUIRKS & FIXATIONS
Targets: topic_fixations, nostalgia_triggers,
         future_orientation_index, detail_granularity
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Q41 | If you are sitting in silence, what random topic will they 
    | inevitably bring up?
Q42 | What specific object, song, or smell instantly launches them 
    | into a story about their past?
Q43 | Are they the kind of person who plans a vacation itinerary 
    | down to the minute, or do they just "wing it"?
Q44 | When they tell a story, do they get bogged down in useless 
    | details (like what color a car was), or rush to the 
    | punchline?
Q45 | What is a conspiracy theory, political opinion, or wild 
    | belief they will debate anyone on?
Q46 | Do they talk more about "the good old days" or what they plan 
    | to do five years from now?
Q47 | What is their absolute favorite hobby or obsession that they 
    | will talk your ear off about if you let them?
Q48 | If they had $1,000 to waste right now, what highly specific 
    | thing would they buy?
Q49 | What is a routine or habit they refuse to break, no matter 
    | what?
Q50 | If you had to summarize their entire worldview in one 
    | sentence, what would it be?
